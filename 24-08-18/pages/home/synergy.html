What is Synergy?

We think of synergy as one person + one person = much more than two people. All non-profits face similar issues and learning curves as they move from startup to mature. Synergy software seeks to accelerate the learning curves of both individuals and organizations.

Sustainable Future Center has experienced significate friction using multiple systems. There are disruptions in switching an accounting or CRM system. A task often needs access to several computer systems with different logins and user interfaces to complete. Synergy seeks to overcome these issues by having an integrated approach that is tailored to meet diverse organizational needs.
